About Mozilla Omaha

Mozilla Club Omaha was started up by Kip (Steven Van Sant) in 2016 to teach web literacy.

Mozilla Club Omaha Manifesto

Mozilla Club Omaha is a small group within the larger network of Mozillians, but nonetheless we pledge to pursue the principles of the Mozilla Foundation. Through our decisions, activities, participation, and contributions we will lead, follow, and build a future for the internet that can be shared by all. We will safegaurd your rights and produce high quality open source resources so that you and others may enjoy, "The web of things"!
